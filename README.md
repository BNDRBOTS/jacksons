Jackson Limousine
Custom web build for Scottsdale and Phoenix private chauffeur operations. Designed for strict accuracy and uncompromised visual fidelity.

Visual Mechanics
Technical Spec: OKLCH color matrix utilizing pure black and titanium contrasts. Hardware-native cursors. WebGL fragment shader calculating liquid depth and pointer tracking.
Client Benefit: The website looks unmistakably premium. Colors stay rich on any screen, and the background subtly reacts to your mouse, making the screen feel alive without being distracting.

Motion
Technical Spec: GSAP batched scroll triggers, magnetic button physics, and hardware-backed transitions.
Client Benefit: Everything glides perfectly. As you scroll, text and images appear naturally without freezing, and buttons gently pull toward your finger or mouse so they are effortless to tap.

Interaction
Technical Spec: Slide-out reservation interface routing directly to dispatch. Accessible input checking with immediate success state handling.
Client Benefit: Booking a car takes seconds. The form slides out instantly without reloading the page, catches typos before you hit send, and tells you the exact moment your request goes through.

Resource Management
Technical Spec: IntersectionObserver throttling for GPU load management, localized DOM measurement caching, and strict reduced-motion fallback compliance.
Client Benefit: The site loads immediately and will not drain your phone battery. It runs flawlessly on older devices and automatically turns off animations if your phone settings prefer a simpler experience.
